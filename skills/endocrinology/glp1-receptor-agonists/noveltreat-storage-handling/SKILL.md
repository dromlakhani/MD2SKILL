# Noveltreat Pre-filled Pen — Storage and Handling

Applies to Noveltreat® Semaglutide Injection Pen strengths **0.25 mg, 0.5 mg, 1 mg, and 2.4 mg** (single-use pre-filled pen).

## Before First Use

- Refrigerate at **2–8 °C** in the original carton.
- **Do not freeze.**
- **Do not use** if the pen is expired, damaged, or the solution is not clear.
- Keep the cap on until ready to inject.

## After First Use

- **Single-use pen — do not reuse.**
- The needle is hidden and the pen auto-locks after use (built-in safety).

## Out-of-Fridge (pre-use) Stability

- Once the cap has been removed (or the pen is taken from the fridge for use), the pen may be kept **unrefrigerated for up to 28 days** at a temperature **not exceeding 30 °C**.

## In-Use Stability

- **Discard** the pen if it has been out of the refrigerator for **> 28 days**.

## Environmental Restrictions

| Exposure | Rule |
|---|---|
| Light | Keep in the original carton; **protect from light** |
| Heat | Keep away from heat; do not leave in a hot car |
| Freezing | Do not freeze; **do not use if frozen** |
| Physical | Do not expose to liquid; do not drop the pen |

## Disposal

- Dispose of used pens **per the doctor's or pharmacist's instructions**.
- Use a **puncture-resistant container** (heavy-duty plastic or equivalent, with a tight-fitting, puncture-resistant lid; upright, stable, leak-resistant, and clearly labelled as hazardous biomedical waste).
- **Do not recycle** the pen.
- **Do not reuse** the pen.
- The **pen cap and empty carton** may be disposed of in household waste.
- Follow **local biomedical-waste regulations**.

## Patient Counseling Points

- Store the unopened pen in the fridge; take it out when you are ready to inject.
- You do **not** have to wait for the pen to warm to room temperature — but you can, if cold injection is uncomfortable.
- Mark the **first-use date** on the carton so you know when 28 days are up.
- If the pen has been frozen — even if it now looks normal — **throw it away**.
- Keep the pen and used sharps container **out of reach of children**.

## Quick Decision Rules

- Pen in the fridge, unopened, not expired → **use**.
- Pen out of fridge ≤ 28 days, cap kept on, ≤ 30 °C → **use**.
- Pen out of fridge > 28 days → **discard**.
- Pen frozen at any point → **discard**.
- Pen dropped or cloudy / discoloured → **discard**.
